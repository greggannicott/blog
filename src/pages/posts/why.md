Why have I started this blog at a time when blogs seem to be dying?

Many reasons actually.

It certainly isn't to make money, to grow a readership or anything like that though.

So, in not particular order:

## I enjoy writing

This one is pretty simple: I enjoy writing. Always have.

I like sitting down and getting things out of my head. But more than that, I like editing and refining it. As much as it can feel like you're hitting your head against a brick wall at times, I enjoy the act of trying to express something, doing a bad job and then finding a way to make it work.

And it feels mad that I don't have a place to do this when it's so relatively easy to put something out there.
